MR PRICE

Mr Price Group Limited, is a publicly traded retail company based in South Africa. Founded in 1985, Mr Price opened in 1987 after the owners bought a controlling interest in John Orrs Holdings, whose trading divisions at the time were The Hub and Miladys.It is a fashion-leading clothing, footwear, cosmetics and accessories retailer that offers on-trend and differentiated merchandise at extraordinary value to ladies, men and kids.
