ABOUT US

Pine City Mall is a destination for the whole family filled with world class shopping, excellent entertainment and several upmarket restaurants.A casual mall with over 230 stores, including global brands, plus a cinema & dining. Ideal for all shopping or family outing needs, with the heart of the mall being the Piazza with the roof that opens creating an indoor / outdoor effect. Eateries to suit all tastes. Nu Metro , Go-Karting and Airsoft range for entertainment.

PIne City Mall can be accessed via two main road entrances.

  1. Entrance 1: Hendrik Potgieter Rd & Christiaan de Wet Rd, Strubens Valley, Pine City, 1735
  2. Entrance 2: Christiaan de Wet Rd & Millennium Boulevard Rd, Strubensvalley, pine City, 1735.
